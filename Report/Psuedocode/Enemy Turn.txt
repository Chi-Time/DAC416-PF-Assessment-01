         - See Moves Special Attack ()
      - ELSE: Does not have enough energy

- IF: User has recharged
   - Pick random choice between Attack, Special Attack or Heal
   - IF: Attack
      - See Moves Attack ()
   - IF: Special Attack
      - IF: Has enough energy
         - See Moves Special Attack ()
      - ELSE: Does not have enough energy
         - Re-prompt choice
   - IF: Heal
      - IF: Has not alread healed
         - See Moves Heal ()
      - ELSE: Has already healed
         - Re-prompt choice
- IF: User has dodged
   - Pick random choice between Attack, Heal or Recharge
   - IF: Attack
      - See Moves Attack ()
   - IF: Heal
      - IF: Has not alread healed
         - See Moves Heal ()
      - ELSE: Has already healed
         - Re-prompt choice
   - IF: Recharge
      - See Moves Recharge ()
- ELSE: User has attacked
   - Pick random choice between Dodge, Attack, or Special Attack
   - IF: Dodge
      - See Moves Dodge ()
   - IF: Attack
      - See Moves Attack ()
   - IF: Special Attack
      - IF: Has enough energy
         - See Moves Special Attack ()
      - ELSE: Does not have enough energy
         - Re-prompt choice
